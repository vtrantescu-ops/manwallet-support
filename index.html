ManWallet Support

Official support page for the ManWallet iOS app.

Need help? Contact us at vt_apps@yahoo.com.

We usually reply within 1-3 business days.

What to include in your message

  • Device model (example: iPhone 14)
  • iOS version
  • App version (from App Store)
  • A short description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce (if possible)

Common topics

  • How to restore Premium purchase
  • How export/import works
  • Reminders and notification setup
  • OCR scanning behavior
